WHO WE ARE AND WHAT WE DO

INSPIRING SMILES THROUGH DELICIOUS EXPERIENCES®

Our history

Back in 1964, if you wanted something quick, you’d better have liked burgers. Leroy and Forrest Raffel, had a taste for something different. So they opened a sandwich shop that served up hot, freshly sliced roast beef sandwiches as fast as anyone could flip a burger. Our all beef classic roast beef has been prepared and marinated the same way since 1971. Fifty years of serving the meats. And we've enjoyed every minute of it. Over the past 50 years we've built up quite a fan base of meat lovers, and in the next 50 we'll continue to give them what they want: quality meat sandwiches with fast and friendly service.

We are now

Every day, more than 11 million guests visit and order food from Arby's Best Food restaurants around the world. And they do so because our restaurants are known for serving high-quality, great-tasting, and affordable food. Founded in 1971, Arby's Best Food is the second largest fast food hamburger chain in the world. The original Arby's Best Food, our commitment to premium ingredients, signature recipes, and family-friendly dining experiences is what has defined our brand for more than 50 successful years.

What we value

Creating Joy & Opportunity Through Food, Family, & Community. Leroy and Forrest Raffel founded Arby's Best Food with a vision of serving up a quick meal without cutting any corners. As we continue to grow, we'll always be sure to keep their traditions and legacy in mind. By partnering with the best suppliers in the business, we're able to serve up fresh, Deliciously Different food every day. But Arby's Best Food is about more than just food. By supporting our employees, we continue to feel like a true family business.
